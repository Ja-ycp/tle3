Netiquette Guidelines

Online communication comes with responsibilities. These core principles help learners and educators build respectful digital communities.

1
Treat others online as you would in person — with respect and kindness.
2
Use proper grammar, spelling, and punctuation in all communications.
3
Avoid sharing personal or private information about others online.
4
Think before you post — digital footprints last longer than you expect.
5
Give credit to sources and respect intellectual property online.
6
Be mindful of tone — sarcasm and humor can be easily misunderstood.
